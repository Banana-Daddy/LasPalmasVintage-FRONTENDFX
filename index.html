Dear shopper,

It started in a thrift bin on Sunrise Way.

Las Palmas Vintage is a lovingly curated collection of preloved womenswear, sourced and styled in the heart of Palm Springs. From sequined 80's blazers to mid-century evening wear, every piece carries the spirit of decades past — the glamour, the craftsmanship, the stories woven into every stitch.

We believe in slow fashion, second lives, and the genuine thrill of finding something extraordinary. Always open online. Always one of a kind.
